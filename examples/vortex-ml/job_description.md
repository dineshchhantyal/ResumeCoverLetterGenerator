# Machine Learning Engineer

**Company:** Vortex AI
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Level:** Early Career

## About Vortex AI

Vortex AI builds machine learning systems that turn large, messy datasets into
reliable predictions our customers can act on. We are a small team that values
rigorous evaluation, clean data pipelines, and models that hold up in
production. As an early career Machine Learning Engineer, you will work
alongside senior engineers to train, evaluate, and ship models that serve real
traffic.

## Responsibilities

- Build and train models in Python and PyTorch, then iterate on them based on
  offline and online evaluation results.
- Design and maintain batch and streaming data pipelines that feed training and
  inference jobs.
- Own model evaluation by building reproducible harnesses, held out splits, and
  slice based metrics that catch regressions before release.
- Perform feature engineering on raw datasets, including cleaning, labeling, and
  documenting reusable transformations.
- Deploy trained models to production on AWS and add monitoring for drift and
  latency to keep inference healthy.

## Requirements

- Proficiency in **Python** and hands on experience with **PyTorch**.
- Experience designing **data pipelines** for training and inference at scale.
- A solid grasp of **model training and evaluation**, including held out splits
  and slice based metrics.
- Practical **feature engineering** skills on real, noisy datasets.
- Experience **deploying models to production on AWS**.
- A BS in Computer Science or a related field, or equivalent experience.

## Nice to have

- Familiarity with Spark and Airflow for large scale data processing.
- Experience serving models behind a FastAPI service.
- Exposure to experiment tracking tools such as MLflow.
- Comfort writing SQL and working in pandas and NumPy.

We are an early career friendly team and will support your growth across the
full model lifecycle, from feature engineering to production monitoring.
