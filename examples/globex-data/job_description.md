# Data Engineer (Early Career)

**Company:** Globex Data
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Level:** Early Career / New Grad

## About Globex Data

Globex Data builds the analytics backbone for fast growing product teams. We move
billions of events a day from source systems into clean, queryable datasets that
power dashboards, experiments, and machine learning. Our small data platform team
owns the pipelines, the warehouse, and the data quality checks that everyone else
depends on, so the work is high leverage and highly visible.

We are hiring an early career Data Engineer to help us build and maintain reliable
ingestion pipelines, model our core datasets, and keep our data trustworthy as we
scale.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain \textbf{ETL} and ingestion pipelines that move data from
  application databases and event streams into our warehouse.
- Write performant \textbf{SQL} and design \textbf{PostgreSQL} schemas and data
  models that downstream teams can rely on.
- Develop pipeline logic in \textbf{Python} and schedule it with workflow
  orchestration tools such as Airflow.
- Stand up \textbf{data quality} checks, monitoring, and alerting so issues are
  caught before they reach stakeholders.
- Operate pipelines on \textbf{AWS}, using services like \textbf{S3} for storage
  and \textbf{Lambda} for lightweight transforms.
- Partner with analysts and ML engineers to deliver well structured, documented
  datasets.

## Requirements

- Strong programming skills in \textbf{Python}.
- Solid \textbf{SQL} and experience with relational databases, ideally
  \textbf{PostgreSQL}.
- Exposure to building \textbf{ETL} or data ingestion pipelines.
- Familiarity with \textbf{data modeling} concepts and clean schema design.
- Hands on experience with \textbf{AWS}, especially \textbf{S3} and
  \textbf{Lambda}.
- Comfort with workflow orchestration (Airflow or similar).
- A bias toward reliable, well tested, monitored systems.

## Nice to Have

- Experience with \textbf{Spark} or other batch processing frameworks.
- Familiarity with containerization using \textbf{Docker}.
- Background in computer science or a related quantitative field.
- An AWS certification.

We review applications on a rolling basis. If you like turning messy data into
dependable datasets, we would love to hear from you.
