# Software Engineer, New Grad

**Company:** Wonka Labs
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Level:** New Grad / Early Career

## About Wonka Labs

Wonka Labs builds delightful software products used by teams around the world. We are a small, fast moving engineering organization that values curiosity, craftsmanship, and collaboration. Our new grads ship real code to production in their first weeks and grow alongside experienced mentors across the stack.

## Responsibilities

- Write, test, and ship features across our backend and frontend systems.
- Collaborate with engineers, designers, and product managers to turn ideas into reliable software.
- Participate in code reviews, learn from feedback, and help raise the quality bar for the team.
- Debug issues in production and contribute to making our services more reliable.
- Learn new tools and parts of the stack as the product and team evolve.

## Basic Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field, completed or in progress.
- Strong computer science fundamentals, including data structures, algorithms, and databases.
- Proficiency in one or more of Python, Java, or TypeScript.
- A genuine willingness to learn and grow across any part of the stack.
- Strong communication skills and a collaborative, team first mindset.

## Preferred Qualifications

- Internship, project, or open source experience building and shipping software.
- Familiarity with web application development, REST APIs, or relational databases.
- Exposure to testing, version control with Git, and CI/CD workflows.
- Leadership or mentoring experience, such as a teaching assistant or club role.

## Level

This is a new grad role. We welcome candidates from any background who are early in their careers and excited to build software well.
