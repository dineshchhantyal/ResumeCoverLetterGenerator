# Backend Engineer

**Company:** Northwind Systems
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Level:** Entry to Early Career
**Team:** Platform Engineering

## About the company

Northwind Systems builds the data infrastructure that powers logistics and supply chain operations for mid market retailers. Our platform ingests millions of inventory and shipment events every day and exposes them through fast, reliable APIs that customer teams build on. We are a small and growing engineering organization, and backend engineers here own services end to end, from schema design to production rollout.

## Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain backend services in Python and FastAPI that power our core platform APIs.
- Model data and write efficient queries against PostgreSQL, with a focus on query optimization and reliability at scale.
- Design and evolve REST and GraphQL APIs that internal and customer facing teams depend on.
- Decompose larger features into well bounded microservices that deploy independently.
- Deploy and operate services on AWS, including containerized workloads and managed data stores.
- Build and improve CI/CD pipelines so that changes ship safely and frequently.
- Add tests and monitoring that keep production incidents low.

## Requirements

- Proficiency in Python and experience building web services with a framework such as FastAPI.
- Hands on experience with PostgreSQL, including schema design and query optimization.
- Experience designing REST or GraphQL APIs.
- Familiarity with microservices and service to service communication.
- Working knowledge of AWS for deploying and running services.
- Comfort with CI/CD and containerized workflows using Docker.
- Strong testing habits and an interest in production readiness.

## Nice to have

- Experience with Redis or other caching layers.
- Exposure to Kubernetes or other container orchestration.
- Infrastructure as code experience with a tool such as Terraform.
- Open source contributions or personal projects that show backend depth.

## What we offer

A high ownership environment where early career engineers ship real services, mentorship from senior platform engineers, and a clear path to growing into broader system design work.
