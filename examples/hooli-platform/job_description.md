# Platform Engineer (Early Career)

**Company:** Hooli
**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Team:** Developer Platform & Infrastructure

## About Hooli

Hooli builds the developer platform that powers every product team in the company. Our mission is to make shipping software fast, safe, and boring. We own the container build pipelines, the Kubernetes clusters, the infrastructure as code that defines our cloud, and the internal tooling that engineers use every day. We are looking for an early career Platform Engineer who is excited to make other engineers more productive.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain container images and Kubernetes workloads that run our internal services.
- Author and review Terraform modules that provision AWS infrastructure in a repeatable, reviewable way.
- Improve our CI/CD pipelines in GitHub Actions to make builds faster and deploys safer.
- Develop internal developer tooling that reduces friction for product engineering teams.
- Improve reliability through better testing, monitoring, and incident reduction across owned services.
- Partition and modernize legacy services so teams can deploy independently.

## Requirements

- Hands on experience with **Docker** and container based workflows.
- Familiarity with **Kubernetes** for deploying and operating services.
- Experience writing infrastructure as code with **Terraform**.
- Working knowledge of **CI/CD**, ideally with **GitHub Actions**.
- Experience deploying to **AWS** (EC2, ECS, S3, or similar).
- Strong scripting and automation skills in Python, Bash, or a similar language.
- A focus on reliability, testing, and production readiness.

## Nice to Have

- Experience migrating services into containerized or Kubernetes based deployments.
- Exposure to gRPC or microservice architectures.
- Experience building internal tools or developer facing platforms.
- Familiarity with caching and queues such as Redis.

## Level

This is an early career role. We welcome new graduates and engineers with up to a few years of experience. We care more about hands on platform and infrastructure work than years on a resume.
