## Status legend

- **submitted**: application form was completed and sent to the ATS.
- **pending**: submitted and waiting on a response (no recruiter contact yet).
- **interview**: at least one interview or screen has been scheduled or held.
- **rejected**: the company declined the application.
- **closed**: the posting was removed or filled before an outcome (no application possible or no decision).
